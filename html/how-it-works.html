How It Works

How Do I Adopt a Pet?

We provide a large collection of pets that can are currently available for adoption. Navigate to the "Search" page to see the most recent colelction that can be filtered for your own needs. One you find a pet that you like, you can click the "Adopt" button to submit an application to the shelter. The shelter will then process your request and will get back to you shortly with next steps.

Am I Guaranteed to be Approved for any Application I Submit?

No, the approval process is at the discretion of the shelters. You may be deneid for various reasons such as your adoption history, you location, a more fitting adopter etc.

Do I Need an Account to Submit an Application?

yes, you must be logged in and have a complete profile in order to submit an adoption application.

Can I Submit a Review of the Shelter?

yes, after you compelte the adoption process, you can submit a review of the shelter to inform others about your experience witht the shelter. You cannot submit a review until you have successfully completed an adoption with the shelter in question.

What Are The Requirements For Adopting a Pet?

Requirements of the adopter vary from shelter to shelter. You can reach out to the shelter you applied to to inquire into their respective policies.

What Is An Adoption Fee?

Adoption fees are used to cover the medical and care of the animal while they wait in the care of the shelter. This covers all costs from 24/7 care, vaccinations, food, health checkups etc.

How Long Will it Take After I Submit an Application?

Processing times vary on a case-by-case basis and will differ due to various variables like the volume of applicants. Processing times take between 1-2 weeks on average.
